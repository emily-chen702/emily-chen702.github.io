Welcome to my secret page!

This page is dedicated to all the foodies out there. One of my favorite things to do is cook/bake food, take picutres of food, and eat food (preferably in that order). Cooking food, especially recipes that I've learned from my grandmother, allows me to stay connected to my culture. Cooking food based off other people's recipes lets me explore their story and memories behind it. Making my own recipes allows me to make my own path, and when I pass it on it will have a whole new meaning. Have a seat, stay a while and browse my attempts at cooking in addition to my favorite places I've been to in Boston!
